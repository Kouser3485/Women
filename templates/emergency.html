Know Your Rights

Empower yourself with knowledge about your rights and the legal protections available to you.

Introduction

Every woman deserves to live with dignity, freedom, and equality. Learn about the rights that protect you against injustice and discrimination.

Your Key Rights

Right to Education

Education is a fundamental right. Schools and universities are mandated to provide equal opportunities for women to learn and excel.

Right to Equal Pay

Women are entitled to equal pay for equal work. Laws prohibit wage discrimination based on gender.

Right Against Harassment

Laws protect women from sexual harassment in workplaces, public spaces, and online platforms.

Right to Privacy

Your personal information and life choices are protected by law, ensuring your dignity and autonomy.
